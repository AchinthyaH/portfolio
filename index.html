A Little More

"I've always believed that the best product work starts with a story worth telling."

I've always been drawn to storytelling — the kind that makes something complex feel inevitable once you understand it. For most of my career, people kept thanking me for something I didn't think much about — explaining things clearly. That kept happening often enough that I started paying attention to it. It's what pulled me toward product. I wanted to do it deliberately: take a piece of technology and make a person feel genuinely connected to it. Not just convinced it works, but glad it exists.

Outside of work, I run tabletop RPG campaigns as a dungeon master — which turns out to be the same skill in a different room. You're still taking something intricate and making people care about it.
